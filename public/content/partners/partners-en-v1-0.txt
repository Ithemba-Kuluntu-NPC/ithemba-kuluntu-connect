SECTION 1 - WHY PARTNER WITH US

Script heading:
Together

Main heading:
Practical impact, carried by local trust

Text:
iThemba Kuluntu is rooted in the communities we serve. Our work is practical, relationship-based and built around real needs on the ground.

Partnerships help us move faster, reach more families and build long-term solutions that communities can trust.

Why partners work with us:

Local trust and grounded delivery

Women-led and community-rooted leadership

Transparent reporting and accountability

Connected programmes across water, food, learning, care and livelihoods

Proven response capacity

Long-term presence in Pondoland
