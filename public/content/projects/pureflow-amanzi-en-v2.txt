tabs.t1.text_1: PureFlow Amanzi uses gravity-fed ultra-filtration technology. Water passes through a membrane with ultra-fine pores of around 0.1 microns. Harmful bacteria and dirt are blocked, while cleaner water flows through instantly without electricity, chemicals, or boiling.

# Tab 2: The Delivery Model
tabs.t2.title: The Delivery Platform
tabs.t2.heading: Last-mile delivery built on trust
tabs.t2.text_1: The real innovation is not only the filter; it is the implementation. We combine local assembly, community-based distribution, WASH education, safe-use training, follow-up visits, ongoing monitoring, and field data collection. This is not a once-off product handover.
tabs.t2.steps: Community mobilisation (engaging traditional leadership), Local team training, Standardised system assembly, Organized community distribution events, Practical WASH education, Interactive safe-use training, Household follow-up visits, Data-driven reporting.

tabs.t3.heading: Safe water also creates work
tabs.t3.text_1: PureFlow Amanzi is designed to create local value, not only deliver a product. This creates practical green work opportunities linked to climate resilience.
tabs.t3.roles: Filter assembly, Distribution support, WASH facilitation, Household demonstrations, Maintenance guidance, Follow-up tracking, Field data collection.

# Tab 4: WASH Education
tabs.t4.title: WASH Education
tabs.t4.heading: Knowledge makes safe water stronger
tabs.t4.text_1: WASH means water, sanitation and hygiene. The filter is the tool, but education is what helps families use it well, trust it, and keep it working. Families receive practical training on why safe water matters, how to clean the system, how to store water safely, and how to prevent contamination after filtration.
tabs.t4.outcomes: Safe water handling, Correct filter care, Clean water storage, Hygiene awareness, Reduced contamination risk, Healthier home routines.
